Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting.

  • Amphiprioninae
  • 11 centimeters
  • 7 years
  • Tropical cordal reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemone fish are among most recognizable of all reef dwellers. They reach about 11 centimeters in length, and are named for multicolored sea anemone in which they make thier homes.
